Welcome to MacroMatic.

MacroMatic is a SPA syntax generator for common macro-supported entertainment applications. Model design has been structured in a way to allow for additional applicaiton-syntaxes to be supported.

The application is still in its early stages of development, and YOU have the opportunity to shape its feature growth.

  • Tracking and Showcasing 'Most Popular Macros'
  • Profile Options that allow for online persistant access to previously generated macros.
  • Sugestions for macro's that have a similar function.
  • Consolidated support pages for listing additional tools that improve gameplay quality of life
  • Other suggestions...
